Staking

Staking funds development — it isn't a yield promise

When you stake $WAN, your tokens are locked into the staking contract. A portion of protocol trading fees plus the Development Treasury allocation are distributed to stakers as rewards; the rest funds engineering, audits, and operations across the three businesses.

Flexible

No lock-up

Base rate

Unstake anytime. Lowest reward weighting, funded purely from protocol fee share. Best for liquidity you may need back quickly.

90-Day Lock

Standard

Base + tier bonus

Tokens locked 90 days. Adds a treasury-funded bonus on top of the base rate and light voting weight on quarterly spend proposals.

365-Day Lock

Builder tier

Highest weighting

Tokens locked 12 months. Highest reward share and full governance weight — this tier is designed for long-term network participants, not short-term speculation.

Where the money actually goes: staking rewards are paid from (a) a fixed share of Raydium trading-fee revenue and (b) the 20% Development Treasury allocation, released on a public vesting schedule. Staking is a governance and incentive mechanism for funding real development work across Wan Constructions, Way Force Digital, and Turbolucent — it is not a guaranteed-return investment product, and reward rates will be published and updated on-chain, not fixed in marketing copy.
Execution Plan

Roadmap

Sequenced so the token and its utility exist only after the legal and technical foundation is in place — not before.

PHASE 0 · FOUNDATION — Q3 2026

Legal & technical groundwork

  • Incorporate the token-issuing entity and complete jurisdictional legal review for a public token offering
  • Independent smart-contract audit of the SPL token and staking contract
  • Multisig treasury wallet setup with named signers published publicly
  • Publish full litepaper, tokenomics, and this site with verifiable company details
PHASE 1 · TOKEN GENESIS — Q4 2026

$WAN deployed, liquidity live

  • Deploy $WAN as an SPL token with fixed 1,000,000,000 supply
  • Seed initial liquidity pool on Raydium from the public-liquidity allocation
  • Launch staking contract with Flexible, 90-day, and 365-day tiers
  • Open Development Treasury dashboard for public, on-chain spend tracking
PHASE 2 · NETWORK ACTIVATION — Q1 2027

Real usage across the three businesses

  • Way Force Digital begins accepting $WAN for retained marketing contracts
  • First Wan Constructions project opened for treasury co-financing
  • Turbolucent ships first agentic-AI automation tooling for partner operations
PHASE 3 · EXPANSION — Q2–Q3 2027

Governance & broader access

  • Activate on-chain governance for treasury spend proposals
  • Evaluate additional exchange listings based on liquidity depth
  • Open development grants funded by the treasury to outside builders on the network
PHASE 4 · MATURITY — 2028 and beyond

Self-sustaining treasury

  • Treasury increasingly funded by protocol fee share rather than initial allocation
  • Governance fully handed to staked-token holders
  • Explore additional verticals under the same network model
Trust & Transparency

What we'll publish, and what we won't promise

Trust here is built on verifiable, checkable things — not marketing language.

Named team & entity

Full legal entity name, registration number, and named founders/directors will be published here before any public raise — not anonymous or pseudonymous.

Independent contract audit

The $WAN token and staking contracts will be reviewed by an independent auditor, with the report linked publicly, before mainnet liquidity goes live.

Public multisig treasury

Treasury and team wallets are multisig, with addresses published so anyone can verify balances and vesting on-chain in real time.

No guaranteed-return language

We won't advertise fixed APYs, price targets, or "guaranteed" returns anywhere on this site — staking rates are variable and disclosed on-chain.

Important notice. $WAN is a utility and governance token, not a promise of profit, dividend, or equity in Wan Constructions, Way Force Digital, or Turbolucent. Cryptocurrency assets are volatile and can lose value, including total loss. Because this raise is intended for participants in multiple countries, WAN Network is committed to completing jurisdiction-by-jurisdiction legal review — including securities-law analysis — before any public sale opens, and the token will not be offered or marketed to persons in jurisdictions where such an offering would be unlawful.
